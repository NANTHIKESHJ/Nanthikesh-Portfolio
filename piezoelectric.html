Piezoelectric Energy Harvester

This project explores energy harvesting from mechanical vibrations using piezoelectric materials. The system converts ambient movement into electrical power, making it ideal for low-power sensors and remote devices.

  • Designed a compact energy harvesting module with optimized piezoelectric placement.
  • Implemented power conditioning and storage for consistent output.
  • Collected real-world performance data across vibration frequencies.
  • Documented the full design and analysis in a presentation slide deck.
